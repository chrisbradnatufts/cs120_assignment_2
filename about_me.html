My Recent Getaway

Last summer during August, I went on a trip with some friends to Tennessee. We went down to Gatlinburg and took some hikes around the Smokies, where I even saw a wild river otter! I explored downtown Gatlinburg and tried the local moonshine and ciders. We stayed in a remote wooden cabin in the mountains. All in all, a great experience for my first time in the US South.

Favorite Restaurant

My favorite restaurant is Bun's House in Medford. It is a Sichuan Chinese food restaurant that used to be called Chili Garden. I love it because they are not afraid of spice, which makes their dishes incredibly authentic and flavorful.

Favorite Food

My favorite dish is Mapo Tofu. I like to eat it served piping hot over a bed of fluffy white rice, which helps balance the intense heat and numbing sensation of the Sichuan peppercorns.
